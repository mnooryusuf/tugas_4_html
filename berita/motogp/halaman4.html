Halaman 4 - MOTO GP

Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along woth teammate Jorge Lorenzo. has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third higest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
